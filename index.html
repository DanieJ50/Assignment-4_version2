Artist Statement

This interactive poem uses Berry Vibes Studio as a playful app concept. Instead of reading a poem from top to bottom, the viewer generates the poem by choosing moods.

Each mood unlocks a CCD recipe and a poetic line. The recipes represent comfort, focus, sweetness, boldness, and growth. The poem changes order through shuffling and scattering, so the layout becomes part of the meaning.
